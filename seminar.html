Seminars of Networked Systems @ IIIS, Tsinghua University

The events are organized by the Networked System Research Group at IIIS in Tsinghua University. It is open to public, and everyone is welcome.

Contents

  • In each seminar, a series of papers on a specific topic would be discussed.
  • All the paper materials (e.g., papers, slides, and summaries) are in English, but most the presentation and discussion would be in Chinese.

NetLab@IIIS Members

    • collecting and assigning papers,
    • setting up the online and offline meeting room,
    • announcing the event 3 days ahead,
    • updating this page.
  • Each group member should take one paper to present.

External Participants

  • You are welcome to listen; if you are willing to present (either your work or a paper), please contact with the organizer for the paper assignment.
  • You can subscribe and unsubscribe by email, and notification would be sent 3 days before the event.
